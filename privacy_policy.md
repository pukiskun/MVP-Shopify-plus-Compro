# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 9, 2026

At our Company Profile & E-Commerce Website ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our customers' personal information. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and secure your personal data in connection with our e-commerce platform and third-party integrations.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

To facilitate order fulfillment and payment processing, we collect the following personal information during the checkout process:
*   **Contact Information:** Name, email address, and phone number.
*   **Delivery Address:** Shipping destination for physical product delivery.
*   **Transaction Information:** Items purchased, total transaction value, and payment status.

---

## 2. Security-First Data Processing

We adopt a security-first approach to protect customer data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration:
*   **Input Sanitization:** All user inputs (such as delivery addresses and contact names) are strictly sanitized on both client and server sides to prevent SQL Injection and Cross-Site Scripting (XSS).
*   **Secure Infrastructure:** All communication between the user's browser, our servers, and third-party APIs is encrypted using industry-standard Transport Layer Security (TLS/HTTPS).
*   **Data Minimization (Credit Card Details):** We do not store or process sensitive credit card, debit card, or bank account information on our servers. All payment transactions are securely handled directly by our payment partner, Xendit.

---

## 3. Third-Party Data Sharing

To fulfill your purchase, we must share limited elements of your personal data with authorized third-party service providers. We only share the minimum amount of data required to complete these services:

### A. J&T Express (Shipping Partner)
*   **Data Shared:** Customer Name, Delivery Address, and Phone Number.
*   **Purpose:** Calculating shipping fees, generating shipping labels, and executing the physical delivery of items.
*   **Data Minimization:** No financial transaction details are shared with J&T.

### B. Xendit (Payment Gateway Partner)
*   **Data Shared:** Transaction details (total price, invoice ID) and contact email/phone number.
*   **Purpose:** Generating secure payment invoices and processing transactions.
*   **Security:** Xendit processes payments under PCI-DSS compliance. We receive only transaction status updates (e.g., SUCCESS, EXPIRED) via authenticated webhooks.

---

## 4. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, request corrections to incorrect data, or request the deletion of your customer record, subject to regulatory transaction-retention requirements.

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
